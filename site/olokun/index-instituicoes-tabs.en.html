Instituto Olokun

Olokun, the "God of the depths of the sea" in Yoruba, invites us on a journey beyond the surface. Like waters that hold secrets in their depths, our mission is to dive into environmental challenges — exploring root causes, identifying patterns, and creating solutions that transform territories and communities.

We are an organization dedicated to coastal preservation and conservation, focused on place-based environmental education and data-driven solutions.

We work with schools, NGOs, city governments, and public institutions to implement projects with measurable social and environmental impact.

Territorial

We understand local contexts and use geospatial data to prioritize actions.

Educational

We invest in educator training and community engagement as the foundation for change.

Evidence-Based

We measure outcomes and validate methods before any scale-up.

Our approach: start small, validate methodology rigorously, and scale with technical and financial safety.

Projects We Can Implement

School Environmental Education

Learning pathways for public and private schools with workshops, campaigns, and participation indicators.

  • Training of multiplier teachers
  • Teaching materials adapted by age group
  • Behavior change assessment

Strategic Territorial Analysis

Use of maps and data to define where to start, which audiences to prioritize, and how to scale efficiently.

  • Territorial prioritization based on objective criteria
  • Coverage analysis and service gaps
  • Phased expansion roadmap

Waste Management in Institutions

Design and implementation of practical routines for segregation, collection, and ongoing education.

  • Initial diagnosis and operational plan
  • Team training
  • Environmental performance indicators

Community Coastal Conservation

Educational projects and local mobilization for conserving sensitive coastal environments.

  • Actions with schools and local leadership
  • Awareness campaigns
  • Social participation indicators
